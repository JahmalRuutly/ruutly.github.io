  • Sales Development Representative

Ruutly is a small-in-stature, mighty-in-mission company, looking for incredible people to join our team. People who hold themselves to the highest standards, who don't take shortcuts, who work hard, who push their comfort-zone, who never work to bare minimums and who show consistency in everything they do.

Ruutly is seeking experienced Sales Development Representatives (SDRs) to drive lead generation activities.

As a SDR, your time will mainly be spent finding and qualifying new business opportunities. You'll continuously execute and optimize our outbound sales campaigns to create qualified sales leads.

At Ruutly You Will:

  • Relentlessly prospect potential leads to generate sales opportunities
  • Contribute to 50 B2B sales your first 6 months
  • Qualify 20-25 quality leads per month for your first 6 months
  • Develop and optimize effective qualifying questions to better understand needs, timing and market trends
  • Use/improve current Ruutly's email campaigns and qualifying call templates
  • Develop new outbound campaigns and help set quotas
  • Make recommendations to the rest of the Ruutly team based on your experience and feedback
  • Represent Ruutly, our ethos and culture to prospective customers
  • Spend 80% of your time on outbound sales development activities and 20% on new tactics

You:

  • You're organized, you keep track of the work you're doing
  • You're motivated to move yourself and your company ahead with the work you do
  • You're consistent with your work and your results
  • You have a quantifiable track record for establishing/hitting quotas
  • You avoid bare minimums
  • You're a clear communicator. You listen as much as you talk and ask meaningful questions to co-workers, customers and prospects
  • You're a team player and don't hesitate to recommend improvements/new ideas
  • You're curious and ask questions. You seek to understand people and processes before objecting or avoiding them.
  • You're able to create genuinely meaningful relationships with potential/existing customers
  • You're hungry to grow with a small company and jump at every opportunity to contribute

Experience:

  • Startup sales experience is a huge plus
  • 1+ years outbound sales experience (preferred)
  • Experience with a CRM (HubSpot preferred)
